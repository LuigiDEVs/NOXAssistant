Frequently Asked Questions

What is NOX Assistant?

NOX is an advanced AI assistant powered by cutting-edge language models and speech recognition technology. It's designed to help you with various tasks through natural conversation.

How do I start using NOX?

Simply click the "Start" button and begin speaking. NOX will listen to your voice commands and respond accordingly. Make sure to allow microphone access when prompted.

What can NOX help me with?

NOX can assist with a wide range of tasks including answering questions, providing information, helping with calculations, and engaging in natural conversations on various topics.

Is my data secure with NOX?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All conversations are encrypted, and we don't store any personal data without your explicit consent. Your security is our top priority.
